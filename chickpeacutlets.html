Chickpea Cutlets

Ingredients

  • 1 Can Chickpeas
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Cup Vital Wheat Gluten
  • 1/2 Cup Bread Crumbs
  • 1/4 Cup Veggie Broth
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 Cloves Garlic (pressed or grated)
  • 1/4 tsp dried rubbed sage
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • Olive Oil for panfrying

Instructions:

  • Mash chickpeas and olive oil together until no whole chickpeas are left.
  • Add remaining ingredients and knead for about 3 minutes, until strings of gluten have formed.
  • Divide dough into four pieces. Knead each piece in your hands for a few moments, then flatten and strech each in a 6 x 4 shape rectangle.(Easier to strech on a flat surface)
  • Add a moderate amt of oil to pan and set it over medium heat.
  • Place cutlets in pan and cook on each side for about 6 - 7 minutes, until lightly browned and firm to the touch.
  • Add gravy and enjoy.
