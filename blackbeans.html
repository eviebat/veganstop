Black Beans and Rice: A Classic

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Cup Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 Cup Green Pepper (chopped)
  • 1 Jalepeno (chopped)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic(minched)
  • 1 Can Black Beans
  • 3/4 Cup of Water
  • 2 tsp Goya Sazon (or whatever seasoning you prefer)
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar

Instructions:

    Easy recipe, makes great leftovers!
  • Heat oil over medium heat and add onion, pepper, jalepeno, garlic and cook until tender ( 8 - 10 minutes )
  • Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl and mix breifly. Once items on the stove are tender, add to the mix.
  • Bring to a boil and then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Once done, remove from heat and mix with rice
