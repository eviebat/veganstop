White Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup White Rice
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 1 tsp Salt

Instructions:

  • Combine rice and water in a saucepan and stir lightly.
  • Bring to a rolling boil.
  • Reduce heat to simmer, cover for 15 minutes while simmering.
  • After 15 minutes(or until water is gone) remove from heat
