Cooking tips:

  • When heating up pil for pan frying, it is easy to tell if the oil is hot enough by dropping a few water droplets in it - if it sizzles, the oil is hot!
  • Add chewy foods to your meals(grilled mushrooms, seared tofu, nuts), they feel more filling and take more time to eat
  • Mix and match - any recipes that call for spices that you don't like, substitue ones you like or just leave them out.
  • Whole nuts are generally more expensive, so if the recipe calls for chopped nuts, buy them that way. Save yourself time and money.
  • Sometimes things don't go as planned but try not to get down on yourself, every new recipe is a learning experience. Relax and have fun!
