Tofu Burger

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 14 ounces extra-firm tofu (1 package)
  • toasted bread
  • thinly sliced carrots
  • spinach
  • spicy mayo

Instructions:

  • Combine soy sauce, sugar and vinegar in a saucepan and cook over low heat until thick.
  • Cut tofu into burgers and marinade them in the sauce for 15 minutes, turning them over halfway.
  • Fry over olive oil.
  • Add tofu burger to bun with carrots, spinach and spicy mayo.
