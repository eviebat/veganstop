Instructions:

    Vegan Bread Crumbs can be hard to find and/or expensive.
  • Place a few slices of bread into the oven at 350 degree.
  • Once bread is very dry and crispy, remove from oven and allow to cool.
  • Stomp it into breadcrumbs.
