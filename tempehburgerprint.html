Tempeh Burger

Ingredients

  • Tempeh
  • Burger Bun
  • 3 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 3 Tbsp Agave Nectar
  • 1/2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, chopped

Instructions:

  • Combine soy sauce, agave nectar, white wine vinegar and garlic to create a marinade.
  • Rub marinade over sliced tempeh and let sit for 15 - 20 minutes.
  • Grill over medium heat on the stove, until lightly brown.
  • Place on bun with favorite condiments

Delicious with Spicy Mayo
