Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp margarine(vegan margarine like earth balance)
  • 2 Tbsp onions(finely chopped)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 3 Tbsp Flour
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 Tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

  • Suate onions and garlic until golden brown.
  • Put margarine in a saucepan pot over medium heat.
  • Once liquid, make a roux by gradually adding the flour to the margarine while continiously stirring to avoid lumps.
  • Add soy sauce, water, and nutritional yeast while stirring.
  • Add garlic, onions and salt and pepper to taste
  • Once desired thickness, remove from heat and add to your favorite recipe
